This is a fansite for GABRIELLA ANNJANE UMALI or more known and endeared to us as "ELLA CRUZ" that provide official updates regularly.Ella Cruz was born on August 17, 1996 in Angat, Bulacan, Philippines. This young lady is a Filipina actress, model, host, product endorser, commercial model and dancer, having been active in the Showbiz Industry since 2006 p to present.

She became popular in the Philippines under ABS-CBN's Star Magic. She was known for playing the teen character of Cristine Reyes in the TV series Dahil Sa Pag-ibig. Cruz was often paired with Francis Magundayao (who played the teen Piolo Pascual in the same TV series). Cruz also become a part GMA Network at the GMA Artist Center from 2005-2006. She recently became popular when an uploaded video of her dancing to "Twerk It Like Miley" turned viral in YouTube.
