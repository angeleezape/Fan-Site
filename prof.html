Gabriella Annjane Umali Cruz (born on August 17, 1996 in Angat, Bulacan, Philippines) is a Filipina actress, product endorser, host, commercial & promotional model and dancer. She is notable as Aryana in ABS-CBN's former TV series Aryana. She became popular in the Philippines under ABS-CBN's Star Magic. She was known for playing the teen character of Cristine Reyes in the TV series Dahil Sa Pag-ibig. Cruz was often paired with Francis Magundayao (who played the teen Piolo Pascual in the same TV series). Cruz also become a part GMA Network at the GMA Artist Center from 2005-2006. She recently became popular when an uploaded video of her dancing to "Twerk It Like Miley" turned viral in YouTube.

She began her professional acting career in 2006 and initially signed with Star Magic in 2007. She signed with them again in 2012 after a hiatus between 2009 and 2012.TRIVIAAs a television host, she was nominated for Best Children Show Host at the 25th PMPC Star Awards for TV in 2011 for being a host in Tropang Potchi.
