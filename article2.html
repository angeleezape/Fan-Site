Ella Cruz releases "Worth It" dance cover!

She showcased her sexy but classy moves to the tune of the Fifth Harmony’s "Worth It". "The dance was choreographed by Macky Quiobe MANILA, Philippines – After her dance covers of Brandon Beal’s "Twerk It Like Miley" and Dawin’s ‘Dessert’ went viral, ABS-CBN actress Ella Cruz has released a new dance video...♥♥

This time, the teen star showcased her sexy but classy moves to the tune of the Fifth Harmony’s ‘Worth It’. The video was uploaded on her official Youtube andFacebook accounts.She was joined by her ‘Twerk It Like Miley’partner and choreographer, Macky Quiobe.With every dance cover, the 19-year-old actress keeps on proving she deserves to be dubbed as the “Teen Dance Princess” by her followers. When she released her dance cover of ‘Dessert’ last August, international singer Dawin noticed it and uploaded it on his Instagram account.

He captioned his post: “@itsellacruz from Philippines! Love this dance you and the dancers did wow. I should crash the party next time ayee” As of posting, the video has more than 450,000 views on Facebook and nearly 500,000 on Youtube.Ella began her career as a child actress in GMA 7. She played he role of the young Sabina in “Majika” (2006) and the young MariMar in “MariMar” (2007).
